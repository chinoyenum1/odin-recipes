Ingredients

  • 5 Avocados - peeled, pitted and mashed
  • 2 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup minced fresh cliantro
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Stir together Avocados and lemon juice in a serving bowl
  2. Add green onion and cliantro
  3. Mix well
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Serve immediately
